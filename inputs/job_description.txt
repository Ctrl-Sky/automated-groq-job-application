What will your typical day look like?

The Deloitte Technology Support team will give you the opportunity to provide IT service management and support to Deloitte’s employees, assist with technology deployments, support client-facing sessions and document processes. You will also have the chance to develop your troubleshooting and organizational skills in a vibrant environment while learning various technologies and being comfortable to talk about them.


The IT Solutions Delivery team is the internal-facing services team of the firm. Our subject matter experts in digital solutions use scalable and innovative technologies to provide efficient responses to day-to-day demands and challenges our business teams are facing, saving our firm time, resources and cost. As a co-op/intern within Solutions Delivery, you could be involved in any aspect of the software development lifecycle or proof of concepts which might include business or system analysis, development & testing, project coordination, as well as research and documentation.


In the Project Management Office (PMO), you will work closely with a wide variety of technology projects to ensure adherence to established project management frameworks. Your responsibilities will include analyzing project data for compliance, supporting project managers with financial tracking and cost control reporting, and overseeing RAID logs to ensure effective risk and change management communication. Additionally, you will maintain documentation for compliance purposes, administer the ServiceNow “Projects” module, and collaborate on Power BI data models.


The Technology & Infrastructure team offers an exciting opportunity to gain hands-on experience within both our Infrastructure Managed Services and Cloud Managed Services teams. The successful candidate will assist with a variety of operational activities, including datacenters, servers, applications, databases, operating systems, networks and meeting room audio/video technologies.
You will also assist in monitoring system performance, supporting incident and problem management processes, and contributing to infrastructure improvement initiatives. The role provides valuable exposure to enterprise-grade IT operations, cloud technologies, and collaboration systems, helping the candidate develop technical, analytical, and teamwork skills in a fast-paced, professional environment.


The Emerging Technologies Delivery Team leads the firm’s journey into newer technologies by delivering and implementing cutting-edge solutions in Generative & Agentic AI, Machine Learning, and Robotic Process Automation. With a strong focus on experimentation, rapid prototyping, and innovation, the team transforms ideas into impactful, scalable solutions. Backed by deep product delivery and management expertise, the team launches engaging systems of differentiation that create lasting business value and drive competitive advantage.


The Technology Business Enablement team is where our technology projects come to fruition. The team works in conjunction with our internal business stakeholders to identify opportunities to leverage technology to solve business problems. The co-op/intern will work with our Portfolio Managers to develop business cases, prioritize demands and ensure that the mechanics of projects are set up correctly to ensure that our internal IT teams are working on the most strategic / highest impact initiatives for the Firm.
