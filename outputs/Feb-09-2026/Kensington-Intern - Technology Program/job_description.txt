About the job
Kensington delivers the world’s most personal travel experiences. We bring to life each client’s desire to travel in a way that’s tailored specifically for them, in the company of local private guides who ensure that they enjoy the authentic best of their destination. The result is a unique journey rich in memories that last a lifetime.

To learn more about Kensington, visit kensingtontours.com.

Technology Program Intern

We are looking for a detail-oriented and organized intern to support active technology projects across our customer engagement and service platforms, including ServiceNow and Twilio.

This role will assist project leads with coordination, documentation, tracking, and operational support activities across multiple in-flight initiatives. The intern will gain hands-on exposure to real enterprise delivery projects involving automation, communications workflows, and platform integrations.

This is an excellent opportunity for someone interested in technology project delivery, platform operations, and cross-functional coordination.

Key Responsibilities 

Support day-to-day project coordination across ServiceNow and Twilio initiatives
Maintain and update Jira boards and ticket tracking with accurate status notes
Assist with meeting minutes, action tracking, and follow-ups
Help maintain project documentation, including:
RACI matrices
Risk and issue logs
Status trackers
Project dashboards
Use Excel to manage trackers, logs, and reporting summaries
Document and organize call flow and process flow diagrams
Support testing coordination and defect tracking activities
Help prepare project status updates and presentation materials
Coordinate with technical and business stakeholders to collect updates
Ensure project artifacts are organized and up to date
Qualifications

Strong working knowledge of Excel (trackers, structured sheets, formulas preferred)
Familiarity with Jira boards or similar ticket/project tracking tools
Clear written communication skills (structured minute-taking)
High attention to detail and organization
Ability to follow structured processes and templates
Comfortable learning new platforms and tools quickly
Able to manage multiple small tasks concurrently
