How you’ll make an impact:

As a student in the IS Development & Application Support (Workflow Management Systems) role, your primary focus will be application support activities and developing code for our internal applications. You will have the opportunity to:

Engage in application support and coding activities while gaining insights into Fidelity's business models and applications.
Undertake ad hoc programming tasks to support our Development Team.
Collaborate with team members in the design, coding, testing, debugging, and implementation of features and functions within our applications and APIs, ensuring adherence to Fidelity's standards and practices.
Participate on documentation initiative to create, enhance and organize supported applications documentation. Evaluate and implement best practices that could be advantageous for the team and our organization.
What we’re looking for:

You are pursuing a university degree in Computer Science, Business with a technical proficiency or a related technical program, ideally in your third or fourth year of study.
You possess a keen interest and understanding of web development and APIs.
You have a strong grasp of Internet programming languages, including Java, .Net, Angular, HTML/HTML5 technologies, and CSS libraries.
Familiarity with scripting languages such as Groovy, Perl, Linux/Unix shell, and SQL/Oracle databases would be beneficial.
Knowledge of Jira, Confluence, SharePoint and ServiceNow would be a plus.
You must be available on a full-time basis for the entire duration of the co-op term.
You will be working on a flexible hybrid schedule as part of Fidelity’s dynamic working arrangement.
