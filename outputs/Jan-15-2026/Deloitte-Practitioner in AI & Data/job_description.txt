About The Team

Our Deloitte Artificial Intelligence & Data services uncover relationships between business insights and organizational intelligence to drive progress for your organization. We do this by leveraging the power of analytics, robotics, and artificial intelligence (AI) to generate the insights that catalyze growth and efficiencies. This helps your organization improve decision-making, increase operational efficiency, mitigate risk, and more.

The Deloitte AI & Data practice has over 350 practitioners across Canada. We provide advisory to our clients to support with:

AI Strategy 
Insights & Engagement 
Data Modernization & Migration 
Engineering, AI & Data Delivery Office / Service Operations 

Within the AI & Data practice, there are different sub-offerings, and you may be able to identify yourself with one or multiple of these.

The AI Strategy sub-offering tackles a range of issues around data governance, privacy, ethics, and the design, enablement and operationalization of AI and Advanced Analytics applications – as well as domain specific applications in supply chain management, customer & marketing, government, financial services, and healthcare. Aside from core skillsets in strategy, design thinking, and digital transformation – the AI Strategy team also comprises of analytics professionals who apply their skills at a strategic level. 
The Insights & Engagement sub-offering leads in identifying use cases, designing and building models, and working with peers to deploy large-scale solutions in data science. Applications include domains of supply chain management, customer & marketing, in government, financial services, energy and resources, consumer, and healthcare industries. 
The Data Modernization & Migration sub-offering help our clients manage the complexity inherent in today’s diverse business landscape while supporting them in consolidating or customizing multiple technologies. You will be part of a practice focused on delivering a breadth of solutions to solve our clients most challenging business problems, with a focus on Integration Platforms, API design, and Micro and Web Services. We help our customers to reduce costs and risks by simplifying integration with existing systems and supporting them in all aspects of creating customized and innovative digital solutions. 
The Engineering, AI & Data (EAI&D) Delivery Office under Service Operations champions quality and mitigating risks in projects supporting the Portfolio Quality leader. This dynamic role supports EAI&D and involves managing scheduling and tracking for key risk management activities, maintaining risk metrics, gaining exposure to what makes a project “successful” and supporting with the development of collateral and materials. The position offers valuable exposure to various stakeholders across the Engineering, AI & Data line of business, participation in critical initiatives, and opportunities to contribute to project success and quality management.
