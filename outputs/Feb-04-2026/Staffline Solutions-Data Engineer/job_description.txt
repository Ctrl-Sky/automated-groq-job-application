About the job
About the Opportunity


At Staffline Solutions, we believe strong data systems power strong decisions.
This Data Engineering opportunity is designed for individuals who want to build, strengthen, or transition into data engineering skills by working on real-world data pipelines and infrastructure challenges.


This role is ideal for learners who want to move beyond theory and gain hands-on exposure to how data is collected, processed, stored, and made ready for analytics and machine learning.


What You’ll Learn & Work On
As part of this Data Engineering upskilling role, you will:
Understand the end-to-end data lifecycle, from ingestion to consumption
Build and maintain data pipelines using Python and SQL
Work with ETL / ELT processes to move and transform data efficiently
Learn data modelling and database design (relational and basic NoSQL concepts)
Gain exposure to cloud-based data platforms and storage solutions
Work with tools such as Apache Airflow, Spark (basics), and data warehouses
Implement data quality, validation, and monitoring practices
Collaborate with mentors on real business use cases used by analytics and data science teams


Who This Is For
We’re looking for individuals who are:
Curious about how data flows behind the scenes
Interested in backend systems, databases, and large-scale data handling
From backgrounds such as:
Computer Science / IT / Engineering
Data Analytics or Data Science aspirants wanting stronger engineering skills
Professionals aiming to transition into Data Engineering
Familiar with basic programming or willing to learn quickly


Prior Data Engineering experience is not required; consistency and a problem-solving mindset are more important.


What You’ll Gain
By joining this opportunity, you will receive:
Structured Data Engineering upskilling and mentorship
Hands-on experience with real, production-style data workflows
A weekly stipend of C$18 throughout the engagement
Strong foundations in data pipelines, cloud concepts, and databases
Exposure to how engineering teams support analytics and business intelligence
Certification/learning documentation (as applicable)
Improved career readiness for Data Engineer, Analytics Engineer, or BI Engineer roles


Our Approach
At Staffline Solutions, we take learning seriously without making it overwhelming.


We believe skills are built through practice
We value ethical, transparent, and supportive learning environments
We focus on long-term career outcomes, not short-term task completion


This role is more than an upskilling opportunity; it’s a chance to build the backbone of modern data systems and step confidently into data-driven careers.
