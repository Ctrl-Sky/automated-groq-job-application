About the job
About the Opportunity



At Staffline Solutions, we don’t just connect talent with roles; we build capability.
This DevOps Engineering opportunity is designed for individuals who want to upskill, transition, or strengthen their foundation in DevOps while working on real-world, business-aligned systems and infrastructure.


Whether you’re a graduate, a working professional looking to pivot, or someone eager to future-proof your career, this role focuses on learning by doing, guided by experienced professionals who are invested in your growth.


What You’ll Learn & Work On

As part of this DevOps upskilling role, you will:



Build a strong foundation in Linux, Networking, and Cloud Fundamentals
Learn CI/CD pipelines and automation best practices
Work with version control systems (Git) and collaborative workflows
Configure and manage cloud infrastructure (AWS/Azure/GCP – fundamentals)
Learn containerisation and orchestration using Docker and basic Kubernetes
Implement Infrastructure as Code using tools like Terraform or CloudFormation
Understand monitoring, logging, and system reliability
Support application deployment, scalability, and performance optimisation
Collaborate with mentors and peers in a structured, supportive environment


Who This Is For

We’re looking for people who are:



Passionate about DevOps, Cloud, and modern software delivery
Willing to learn, experiment, and grow
From backgrounds such as:
Computer Science / Engineering
Information Technology
Networking / Systems Administration
Software Development
Career switchers with a strong technical or problem-solving mindset
Comfortable with basic scripting (Bash/Python) or eager to learn from scratch


Prior professional experience in DevOps is not mandatory; commitment, curiosity, and consistency matter more.

By joining this opportunity, you will receive:
