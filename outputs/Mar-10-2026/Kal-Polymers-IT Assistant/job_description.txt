Position Description:	
COMPANY OVERVIEW:
For 30 years, Kal-Polymers has been an optimistic & efficient workplace with a multinational presence. Corporate culture is at the core of every organization and heavily influences its success - positivity and a continuous improvement mindset are necessary for candidates. While other organizations have slowed or been shut down as a result of Covid-19, we've been continuing our growth trajectory beyond our expectations. KP is a plastics compounding company providing clean post-industrial recycled/upcycled material to many industries.

Position Overview:
We are seeking a driven and detail-oriented IT Assistant to support the development and maintenance of interactive web applications for our manufacturing operations. This role will focus on both our existing manufacturing dashboard system and new 3D visualization tools that help operators monitor equipment, follow standard operating procedures, and maintain quality standards. The ideal candidate will have experience with modern JavaScript frameworks, 3D visualization libraries, data visualization, and responsive UI development. This role requires collaboration across departments to identify and implement solutions that enhance operational efficiency and training effectiveness.



Job Duties and Responsibilities:
• Develop and maintain interactive web applications using React, Three.js, and Tailwind CSS to visualize manufacturing equipment and processes
• Build and optimize 3D model visualization tools that highlight critical components and inspection points
• Implement guided tour functionality to assist operators in conducting quality inspections and following SOPs
• Maintain and extend our existing React-based manufacturing dashboard that visualizes real-time machine data
• Optimize and maintain monitoring views for different equipment types including extruders, grinders, and blenders
• Maintain interfaces to monitor maintenance requests and equipment downtime
• Build shipping and receiving tracking components to improve logistics visibility
• Enhance laboratory testing and quality control monitoring interfaces
• Integrate video content for training and demonstration purposes within the applications
• Design responsive user interfaces that work effectively in manufacturing environments
• Create interactive dashboards that track inspection progress and maintenance activities
• Implement state management solutions to handle complex application behaviors
• Conduct testing, debugging, and performance optimization to ensure smooth application performance
• Collaborate with different departments to understand equipment specifications and operational requirements
• Document code and application features to support ongoing maintenance and development
