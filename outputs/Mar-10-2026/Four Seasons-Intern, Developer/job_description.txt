Role Summary:

The Intern Developer (Full Stack - TypeScript) supports the Digital Experience Team within Commercial Technology. This role contributes to the design, development, testing, and support of digital, eCommerce, and AI-enabled solutions across web, mobile, and backend platforms. Working within an Agile delivery pod, the intern will collaborate with engineers, product managers, and business stakeholders to build scalable, secure, and high-performing applications that enhance guest and staff experiences.



Key Activities:



Digital Experience Delivery

Support development of digital, eCommerce, and AI-enabled solutions across web and backend platforms.
Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented TypeScript code.
