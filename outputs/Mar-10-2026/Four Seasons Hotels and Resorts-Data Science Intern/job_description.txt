Data Science Intern - 2026 Four Seasons Corporate Summer Internship



Department Overview:

The Data Science team sits within the Technology Innovation & Data (TID) organization and supports enterprise-wide needs across Personalization, Guest Experience, Digital, CRM and more. The team builds machine learning models, AI prototypes, and analytics solutions that advance personalization and strengthen decision science across the organization.



Role Summary:

As a Data Science Intern, you will contribute to the development of AI/ML prototypes and data science solutions that support Four Seasons' strategic priorities. Working closely with Data Scientists and cross functional partners, you will explore datasets, build and test supervised and unsupervised learning models, perform feature engineering, support NLP workflows, and help evaluate model performance.



This internship is ideal for students who want hands-on experience applying modern modelling techniques in a real business environment using tools such as Python, MLflow, Azure Databricks, and large scale cloud data platforms.



Key Activities:



AI/ML Model Improvements and Prototyping

Learn the architecture, purpose, and performance characteristics of existing production machine learning models used across Commercial and enterprise initiatives, with guidance from Data Scientists.
Assist in evaluating current model inputs, feature sets, and performance metrics to identify areas for incremental improvement.
Support the enhancement of existing production models by experimenting with updated features, refined model parameters, or alternative algorithms, following established documentation and coding standards.
Contribute to research, innovation, and rapid prototyping of AI use?case opportunities, including the design and refinement of evaluation frameworks.


Data Wrangling, Exploration & Visualization

Clean, structure, and validate datasets in collaboration with subject matter experts to ensure high?quality modeling inputs.
Apply NLP techniques to analyze unstructured text data (e.g., call transcripts, chats) to extract signals and insights.
Perform exploratory analysis to identify trends, anomalies, and model?relevant features.
Create visualizations and present findings to technical and non?technical stakeholders.


Collaboration & Standards

Work closely with Data Engineering to validate data pipelines and ensure they meet model requirements.
Work closely with Data Governance to complete data cataloging and business glossary activities.
Follow internal coding guidelines, documentation standards, and delivery methodologies.
Participate in peer code reviews and collaborate with third?party partners where appropriate.
