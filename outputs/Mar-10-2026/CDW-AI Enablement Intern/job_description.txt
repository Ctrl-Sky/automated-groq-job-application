AI Enablement Intern – Summer Internship Program

CDW is committed to becoming an AI‑fluent organization, where technology enhances—rather than replaces—human creativity, judgment, and decision‑making. As an AI Enablement Intern, you will support teams across the organization as they explore, adopt, and responsibly apply AI, automation, and analytics tools in their day‑to‑day work.
This is a hands‑on, cross‑functional internship designed for students who are curious about AI, eager to learn, and excited to help teams work smarter at scale. Interns will be deployed against real business initiatives across Finance, Sales, Services, Marketing, Operations, Customer Success, Cybersecurity, Purchasing, and IT.
What You’ll Do: 

As an AI Enablement Intern, you may contribute to initiatives such as:
AI & Automation Enablement

Support the design, testing, and documentation of AI‑enabled workflows, agents, dashboards, and automation solutions
Assist with building prompt libraries, Copilot workbooks, and reusable automation assets to support common business tasks
Help identify and deliver “quick win” automations (e.g., reporting, data entry, document generation, process handoffs)
Analytics, Reporting & Data Readiness

Support analytics initiatives including financial modeling, spend analysis, pipeline and renewals tracking, investment tracking, and operational reporting
Assist with dashboards and reporting using tools such as Power BI, Salesforce reports, or AI‑generated summaries
Contribute to data readiness and integrity efforts by helping standardize files, improve metadata, clean legacy content, and support governance practices that enable effective AI usage
Business Process & Workflow Optimization

Document current‑state workflows and identify opportunities for automation across areas such as:
Sales operations, proposals, SOWs, and renewals
Customer Success journeys and operational playbooks
Managed Services, Cybersecurity, and Risk Advisory documentation
Purchasing governance, compliance tracking, and freight reporting
Support AI‑assisted documentation, QA, and report generation to reduce manual effort and improve consistency
Customer & Partner‑Facing AI Initiatives

Support internal and client‑facing AI initiatives, including prototyping, testing, and enablement activities
Assist with AI‑enabled customer success motions, journey orchestration, and operational signals
Contribute to marketing, ecommerce, and partner initiatives that leverage AI for seller productivity, content discovery, and digital experience improvements
Enablement, Adoption & Knowledge Sharing

Help create training materials, demos, user guides, and adoption resources for AI tools and automations
Document use cases, best practices, success stories, and lessons learned to support organizational learning
Collaborate with stakeholders to gather feedback and continuously improve AI solutions
Promote responsible, transparent, and ethical use of AI in alignment with CDW guidelines
What we expect of you: 

Currently enrolled in a full-time post‑secondary program, with the expected graduation date of Dec 2026 or May 2027
Strong curiosity about AI, automation, and analytics and how they can be applied in real business environments
Comfort working with data, documentation, and process‑oriented work
Strong communication skills and the ability to collaborate with both technical and non‑technical partners
Organized, adaptable, and able to manage multiple tasks or projects
A learner’s mindset with a willingness to experiment, ask questions, and iterate
Familiarity with tools such as generative AI, low‑code/no‑code platforms, analytics, automation, or business systems is an asset
Please note this internship will take place from May 2026 to August 2026

What You Can Expect: 

Exposure to real, high‑impact AI and automation initiatives across a large, complex organization
Meaningful work tied to measurable business outcomes and operational efficiency
Mentorship and collaboration with professionals across business and technology teams
Opportunities to build practical experience in AI enablement, analytics, automation, and change management
A supportive, inclusive culture that values learning, innovation, and teamwork
A strong early‑career foundation and potential pathway to future opportunities at CDW
