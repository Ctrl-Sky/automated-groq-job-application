About the job
About Bree

Bree is a consumer finance platform that brings better, faster, and cheaper financial services to over half the Canadian population who live paycheck to paycheck. We operate in a huge, but overlooked market in a country with the least amount of financial technology innovation in the developed world. Our first act is to become the cheapest and best provider of short-term credit to the 20 million people in Canada who live paycheck to paycheck.

More than half a million Canadians have already signed up with Bree, and we believe we are just scratching the surface. We are in an exciting place where we have product market fit, explosive growth, and a clear path to becoming one of the most important FinTechs in Canada.

We have $5M ARR per full-time engineer, growing at a double-digit monthly rate, profitable, and have had 0 voluntary employee churn. We were part of Y Combinator in 2021 and raised a $2M seed round shortly after.

About The Internship

Bree is on a mission to build the best AI-native team across engineering, product, design, operations, and marketing. Our internship program is designed to give you the chance to explore impactful projects across multiple functions while working alongside some of the best minds in FinTech.

Instead of limiting you to one department, we’ll match you to opportunities based on your skills, interests, and career goals. You’ll have the chance to contribute directly to building products, solving hard problems, and shaping the future of consumer finance in Canada.

We are open to 4, 8, and 12-month co-op terms.

What You'll Do

Depending on your team and interests, you may:

Engineering: Contribute to scalable systems, build new features, and leverage AI tools to ship faster and smarter.
Product & Design: Support user research, design experiments, and help create intuitive financial products.

Across All Roles, You’ll

Take ownership of real projects from day one.
Collaborate with teammates across multiple disciplines.
Learn and apply cutting-edge tools, including AI-first workflows.
Gain experience in a hyper-growth, high-performance startup environment.

What We're Looking For

Current student or recent graduate in any field (all majors welcome).
Curious, adaptable, and motivated to learn quickly.
Strong communicator who enjoys solving problems in teams.
Excited about FinTech, AI, and building for millions of users.
A self-starter who thrives in fast-paced, high-growth environments.
