Intern, Inegrations & AI - 2026 Four Seasons Corporate Summer Internship



Role Summary:



We have an exciting opportunity for an Intern, Integrations & AI to support the development of an AI-powered agent that enables Four Seasons staff to access enterprise knowledge quickly and confidently.



In this role, you will work with teams using cutting-edge AI and integration technologies, helping to improve the accuracy and reliability of AI responses through prompt engineering, while also creating clear technical documentation that supports sustainability and scale. You will have exposure to real enterprise systems and the opportunity to engage with stakeholders and end users as part of AI enablement and outreach.



This internship is a unique opportunity to work within Four Seasons' Technology Innovation & Data group, partnering with teams building innovative solutions that enhance how our global workforce finds and uses knowledge. The intern will contribute meaningfully to a solution designed for real end users-and see how AI enablement and adoption is delivered in a global luxury hospitality organization.



Key Activities:

Assist with prompt engineering and tuning to improve the quality, accuracy, and consistency of AI-generated responses.
Help design and maintain prompt libraries, test scenarios, and evaluation approaches to identify gaps and improvement opportunities.
Document integrations, workflows, and data flows, translating technical designs into clear, maintainable documentation.
Support the creation of operational guides, runbooks, and reference materials to help sustain and scale AI solutions.
Collaborate with engineers, architects, and product stakeholders to understand requirements and reflect them accurately in prompts and documentation.
Participate in working sessions with business users to gather feedback and incorporate learnings into prompt and workflow improvements.
Contribute to AI enablement and outreach efforts, including user guides, FAQs, and demo support.
