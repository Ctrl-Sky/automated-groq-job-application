Position Title:	Software Quality Assurance Developer Intern (42)
Position Description:	
Position Summary:

The successful candidate will join our Quality, Warranty, Verification & Validation organization. The candidate will have strong analytical skills, excellent communication skills, and a passion for improving software engineering practices. The candidate will be assigned to a project that is building a cutting-edge system to analyze, using AI, quality issues for our product, categorizing, root causing and providing insights to the Quality Assurance engineers and management.



Work Location: Waterloo, Onsite

Work Term: 4 Months

Develop features for dynamic inputs using state of the art technologies such as RAGS and MCP to expand the scope of the LLM models
